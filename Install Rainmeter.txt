R A I N M E T E R

To install Rainmeter, you have to do the following steps:

1. Download Rainmeter from https://www.rainmeter.net/
2. Choose a skin from the page https://visualskins.com
3. As a sugestion, install Mond and MonterreyRainmeter
   - Mond: https://visualskins.com/skin/mond
     Clic on 'Download'
   - Monterrey Rainmeter: https://github.com/creewick/MontereyRainmeter/releases
     Clic on the link 'Monterey_1.0.0.rmskin'
